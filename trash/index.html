Trash duty includes:

Waste inside the house

  • Remove trash bag from bin and replace it when it is full.
  • Dump paper bag of recyclables in bin when it is full. You can reuse the same bag unless it's soiled.
  • Take out compost and replace bag with green compostable bag when it is full.

Remembering to put out the bins

  • Put the cans on the curb and bring them back in.
  • Trash and recycling on Sunday night and Wednesday night.
  • Compost on Wednesday night.
